The East Asia connection

Since 2021, Vestano International has cultivated a high-performance trade corridor across East Asia. We specialize in sourcing from the world's leading manufacturing powerhouses, China and Taiwan, to bring world-class retail technology and industrial hardware to the GCC and Indian markets.

Precision imports from Taiwan

  • High-End Computing & POS Hardware: Sourcing advanced, fanless POS terminals and industrial-grade touchscreens known for longevity and processing power.
  • Precision Thermal Technology: High-resolution label printers and specialized thermal mechanisms for demanding warehouse environments.
  • Smart Retail Components: Cutting-edge RFID modules and advanced scanning technology that streamline automated inventory management.

The Vestano trade edge

  • Direct Factory Access: Direct links with tech manufacturers in Taiwan and industrial hubs in China help clients access the latest hardware iterations early.
  • Technical Compliance: Imports from Taiwan and China are vetted for international electronics standards and smooth Edge ERP integration.
  • Optimized Supply Chain: From sea freight through Ningbo to air freight from Taipei, we manage logistics so tech upgrades arrive on schedule and within budget.
